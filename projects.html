Projects

A sample of ongoing and past projects at the intersection of economics, data science and policy.

Environmental Regulations & Agricultural Trade

Econometric analysis of whether stricter EU environmental policy stringency leads to carbon leakage through changes in agri-food imports from major trading partners.

Real-Time Migration Pressure Index (RMPI)

Machine learning index combining climate, conflict and food security data to track and predict migration and displacement pressure at subnational level.

Digital Health & the Digital Divide

Research assistant work on TB-focused digital health projects, examining how digital divides, trust and health worker facilitation shape technology adoption.

Economic Complexity & Renewable Energy

Panel-data and time-series analysis of the link between economic complexity and renewable energy consumption in South Asian economies.
